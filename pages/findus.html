Find Us

By train or coach to Milton Keynes Central (MKC). Then one of the following.

By cycle

Assuming you are coming from the Milton Keynes Central station, the best way to get to The Open University is definitely by bike. If you brought your own, wonderful! If not, there are Santander bikes right outside the station. They can be hired there and returned either near the university or after you get back to the MKC station. The map of the bikes and availability is here: Santander Cycles MK. Notice that you will need to register before using this service -- thankfully, it is relatively pain-free.

It is advisable to stay on the redways (shared paths for bikes and pedestrians). Google Maps knows Milton Keynes well and if you search the route from the MKC to The OU by bike, it will suggest a path on the redways. Expect to cycle 6km (or 3.72822715mi or 6561.68yds or 19685ft, phew!) and circa 30mins. Have a look at this route suggestion.

Bike storage: If you come on a bike (cycle) -- whether your own or Santander's, we have a secure, sheltered, and lockable place to store your 2-wheeled friend. Do let us know and we will get you sorted.

By bus

Assuming you are coming from the Milton Keynes Central station and it is pouring down rain, the best way to get to The Open University is by bus. You have several options.

  1. OU bus: in the morning until 10:30 and in the afternoon from 4:30pm there is a special bus that takes you (without stopping) between MKC and The OU. See here. The price is £1.
  2. Buses 11/11A/12/12A all go from the MKC station to The OU. The price is £2-3 for students and a bit more for "adults". See here for more info.

By Uber

Assuming you are coming from the Milton Keynes Central station and it is pouring down rain and you are injured, the best way to get to The Open University is by an Uber. There are a few in the city. The price is around £7.50.

By Taxi

Assuming you are coming from the Milton Keynes Central station and it is pouring down rain and you are injured and you lost your smartphone, the best way to get to The Open University is by Taxi. The price is around £12. You can find them outside the MKC station. More information for this kind of transport to The OU can be found here.
