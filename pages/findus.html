Find Us

Assuming you are coming from the Milton Keynes Central station, the best way is definitely by bike. There are Santander bikes right outside the station. They can be hired there and returned either near the university or after you return back to the station. The map of the bikes and availability is here: Santander Cycles MK. Notice that you will need to register before using this service -- it is relatively pain-free.
